mantra design, sept 2020
typographic composition

creating a series of merchandise items from a personal mantra
of your choice, as well as translating the mantra design into dynamic content

the inspiration of this mantra came from my mother.

as she would greet any guest with kindness and make them
feel welcomed and relaxed.


this was also the reason behind incoporating both Chinese and English letters.

The Chinese letter 家 ("jia") incoporated in this design means "home".

I made the letter intertwined with each other to show the influence of both culture to me.

Design made in Photoshop and Illustrator
