the metamorphosis, dec 2020
book design

the metamorphosis is a short novel written by Franz Kafka.

the book focuses on the themes of isolation, identity, and
pressure from society.

the relationship between light and dark is a major motif in the book.

therefore, the chapter pages are cropped to build up tension
towards the climax and slowly revealing the "light" near the end.

the book contains 94 black and white pages in total, printed and
bound by hand in the design lab at The New School.

the use of grayscale and gradient effects allow the reader to further
experience the transition from darkness to light, along with the
progression of the story.

dimensions: 5"x8"
